Santo Tirso, Porto, 27 November 2020
JFO//MSP
14-year-old in serious condition after being run over by bus in Santo Tirso
A 14-year-old boy was in serious condition today after being run over by a bus in Vila das Aves, in the municipality of Santo Tirso, a Civil Protection source told Lusa.
The young man, after being assisted at the scene, was transported by the Vila das Aves Fire Department to Hospital São João, in Porto, added the source.
The accident occurred at 6:16 pm, on Rua Luís Gonzaga Mendes de Carvalho.
On site, providing assistance, in addition to the Vila das Aves Firefighters, was the Vila Nova de Famalicão Emergency and Resuscitation Medical Vehicle, the Santo Tirso Immediate Life Support Ambulance and the local GNR, a source from the corporation.
