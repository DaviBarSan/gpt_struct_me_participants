Mogadouro, Bragança, 30 November 2020
FYP//RBF
48-year-old man dies in work accident in Mogadouro
A 48-year-old man died today as a result of a work accident in the town of Vilarinhos dos Galegos, municipality of Mogadouro, district of Bragança, a fire department source told Lusa.
"This is an accident that involves a fall from a height. The 48-year-old man worked on a construction site. The victim ended up succumbing to his injuries, ending up dying while being transported by ambulance to the football field , where she would later be helitransported to a reference hospital", explained the Mogadouro fire commander, José Carrasco.
The alert was given at around 5 pm.
The relief operations involved Mogadouro firefighters, the Immediate Life Support ambulance (SIV) and the Emergency and Resuscitation Medical Vehicle (VMER) from the National Institute for Medical Emergency (INEM).
The INEM helicopter parked in Macedo de Cavaleiros was also activated
The Mogadouro GNR took charge of the incident.
