Plane makes emergency landing at Lisbon airport due to engine failure
A plane made an emergency landing today at Humberto Delgado Airport, in Lisbon, due to an engine failure, a Public Security Police source told Lusa.
The alert for an emergency landing due to the aircraft having a stopped engine, according to the same source, was given at 5:50 pm.
The aircraft landed at 6:32 p.m., without any notable incidents, he added.
The plane belongs to the airline Jota Aviation and was connecting the airports of Farnborough, England, and Lisbon, according to the ANA-Aeroportos de Portugal website.
