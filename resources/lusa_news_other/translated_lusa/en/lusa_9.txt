Vila do Conde, Porto, 24 November 2020
JFO // ACG
GNR arrests sentenced to prison during road inspection in Vila do Conde
A 38-year-old man, sentenced to a sentence of six years and six months in prison, was detained by the GNR in Vila do Conde in possession of drugs, that police force revealed today.
In a statement, the GNR states that the arrest took place on Sunday during a road inspection action, having seized, after searching the vehicle, "12 doses of heroin and nine doses of cocaine".
Once the suspect was identified, the GNR found that he had "a criminal record for drug trafficking, theft and robbery".
The Guard also verified that the man "had an outstanding warrant for his consignment to a prison issued by the Judicial Court of Vila do Conde, to serve a sentence of six years and six months in prison."
The detainee was taken to the Porto Prison and the facts were sent to the Judicial Court of Vila do Conde, according to the press release.
