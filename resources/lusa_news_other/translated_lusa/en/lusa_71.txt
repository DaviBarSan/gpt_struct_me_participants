Lisbon, 03 Nov 2020
AFE // NFO
Man invades Estádio da Luz facilities with car
A man broke into the facilities of the Estádio da Luz, in Lisbon, this afternoon with a car, a source from the Lisbon Metropolitan Command (Cometlis) of the PSP told the Lusa agency.
According to the police, “a man entered with a vehicle inside” the Estádio da Luz, in the parish of São Domingos de Benfica, and the incident occurred at 3:00 pm.
The driver was eventually detained, but the PSP explained that the arrest may not be related to this infraction, without providing further information.
The news was reported by Rádio Renascença, which reports that the man drove the car onto the lawn.
The police said they did not have information to confirm how far the driver went inside the Benfica stadium facilities.
Cometlis sent additional clarifications about this man's arrest for later.
