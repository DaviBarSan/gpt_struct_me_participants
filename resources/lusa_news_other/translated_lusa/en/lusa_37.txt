Belmonte: GNR seizes weapons for domestic violence
The GNR seized firearms and charged a 47-year-old man, suspected of committing domestic violence against his ex-partner, in the municipality of Belmonte.
In a statement sent to the Lusa agency, the GNR Territorial Command of Castelo Branco explains that the operation was carried out on Tuesday, through the Belmonte and Caria posts.
“As part of an investigation into domestic violence, the military found that the suspect, a 47-year-old man, repeatedly exercised physical and psychological violence, through physical attacks, threats using a firearm and verbal insults to the victim, his ex-partner, aged 33, with an escalation of violent behavior, due to abusive alcohol consumption”, is mentioned in the note.
According to the GNR, two house searches were carried out and three compressed air rifles, a shotgun, a reproduction firearm, a compressed air pistol, a spring-loaded knife and various ammunition of different calibers were seized.
The suspect was named a defendant and the facts were referred to the Covilhã Judicial Court.
