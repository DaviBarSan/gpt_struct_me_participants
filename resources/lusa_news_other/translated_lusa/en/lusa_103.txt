PJ investigates violent death of 60-year-old man in Celorico de Basto
The Judicial Police is investigating the death of a man aged around 60 found in Gandarela, Celorico de Basto, in the district of Braga, with signs of serious head injury, a police source confirmed today.
A source from the National Institute of Medical Emergency (INEM) told Lusa today that the alert for the Gandarela case, in the municipality of Celorico de Basto, involving a person found with severe trauma, for an unknown reason, was given at 9:45 pm on Sunday .
He added that the first rescuers to arrive at the scene, from the local Red Cross, found him already in cardiorespiratory arrest.
