GNR of Leiria detects illegal discharge of pig effluents
The GNR of Leiria announced today that it detected an illegal discharge of pig effluents into the soil, in the parish of Bidoeira de Cima, in the municipality of Leiria, after a patrolling action.
The GNR Territorial Command, through the Leiria Environmental Protection Center, informs that, on November 27th, it detected an illegal discharge of livestock effluent into the soil, originating from an agricultural holding.
GNR soldiers were on patrol when they were confronted with "livestock effluents flowing freely into the ground directly from a livestock effluent storage lagoon, without any type of mechanism to ensure their purification", says a press release .
After some due diligence, the point of origin of the discharge was identified, resulting in the preparation of an administrative offense report for discharging livestock effluent directly into the ground, punishable by a fine of a maximum value of 144 thousand euros, the note adds.
