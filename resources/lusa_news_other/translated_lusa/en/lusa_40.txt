Vila Real de Santo António, Faro, 19 November 2020
MAD//MCL
Almost four tons of hashish seized from the Guadiana River - GNR
The Coastal Control Unit seized almost four tons of hashish and a high-speed vessel this morning on the Guadiana River, in the Algarve, reported the GNR.
In a statement, the GNR said that 115 bales of hashish weighing 3,785 kilos were seized and a high-speed vessel, detected near the mouth of the Guadiana River with “suspicious behavior”, was later intercepted near Foz de Odeleite.
The GNR soldiers were unable to intercept the suspects, who “fled to Spanish territory, and the Spanish police authorities were informed”, the note also reads.
According to the GNR, the vessel was detected with the help of the Integrated Surveillance, Command and Control System (SIVICC), as part of a coastal surveillance and patrolling action.
The facts were sent to the Judiciary Police (PJ).
