Celorico da Beira, Guarda, 22 December 2020
ASR // JEF
Man dies in motorcycle crash in Celorico da Beira
A 34-year-old man died today, on Estrada Nacional 17, in Celorico da Beira, in the district of Guarda, following the crash of the motorcycle he was driving, civil protection and GNR sources told the Lusa agency.
According to a source from the Guarda District Relief Operations Command (CDOS), the alert for the accident was given at 2:28 pm.
Source from the Territorial Command of GNR da Guarda told Lusa that the motorcycle was traveling on Estrada Nacional 17, in the direction of Celorico da Beira - Cortiço da Serra, and the crash occurred “a few meters before the town of Cortiço da Serra”, which belongs to the municipality by Celorico da Beira.
The death of the motorcycle driver was confirmed by the doctor from the Emergency and Resuscitation Medical Vehicle (VMER) who went to the scene.
“Apparently it was a mistake,” said the source, indicating that the causes of the accident are being investigated by the GNR’s Road Accident Crime Investigation Unit.
Five vehicles and ten members of the Celorico da Beira Volunteer Fire Department, the VMER da Guarda and the GNR were at the scene of the accident, according to a CDOS source.
