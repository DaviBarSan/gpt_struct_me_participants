Braga, October 26, 2020
VCP // JAP
MP accuses man of abusing his partner's daughter in Braga and Amares
The Public Prosecutor's Office (MP) of Braga accused a man of aggravated sexual abuse of children, the victim being his partner's daughter, the Porto District Attorney General's Office announced today.
In a note published on its page, that prosecutor's office states that the abuse began in 2017, at a time when the victim was 10 years old.
The MP considered that the defendant started living with his partner in 2016, first in Braga and then in Amares.
According to the MP, the defendant, between 2017 and 2019, “maintained sexual relations” with his partner’s daughter, “taking advantage of the moments he spent alone with her”.
The indictment order is dated October 5th.
