Paços de Ferreira, October 13, 2020
APM // MSP
Covid-19: GNR de Freamunde, Paços de Ferreira, closed a restaurant that brought together 50 people
The GNR of Freamunde announced today that it had closed a restaurant and drinks establishment that brought together around 50 people, in that location in the municipality of Paços de Ferreira.
The military detected that the establishment was operating with live music, which occurred "following an inspection action to comply with the rules relating to the covid-19 pandemic", according to a statement from the authority.
The 48-year-old owner was identified and the order was given to end the event, resulting in the preparation of an administrative offense report.
