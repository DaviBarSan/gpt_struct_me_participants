Borba, Évora, 20 December 2020
TCA // EL
GNR arrests drug trafficking suspect in Borba
The GNR detained a 31-year-old man on suspicion of committing the crime of drug trafficking in the city of Borba, Évora district, a source from that security force told the Lusa agency today.
According to the same source, the man was detained by military personnel from the Estremoz Territorial Detachment on Saturday and will be presented to the Vila Viçosa Court on Monday for first interrogation and possible application of coercive measures.
The GNR seized 24.3 grams of heroin from the suspect, a digital scale, scissors, plastic bags for storing individual doses and around 150 euros in cash.
