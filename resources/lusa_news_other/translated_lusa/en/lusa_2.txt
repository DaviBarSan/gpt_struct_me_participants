Porto, 15 Oct 2020
JFO // ACG
PSP arrests drug trafficking suspect and seizes firearm in Porto
A 20-year-old man suspected of drug trafficking was arrested by the PSP in the Pasteleira Nova neighborhood, in Porto, and heroin, cocaine and a firearm were also seized, that police force reported today.
In a statement, the Porto Metropolitan Command of the PSP clarifies that the arrest took place on Wednesday, during a police action to combat the crime of drug trafficking.
During a home search, the PSP seized enough heroin for around 599 individual doses and enough cocaine for around 235 individual doses from the unemployed person.
A firearm, four rounds of ammunition, a tablet, a speaker and 222 euros in cash were also seized, according to that police force.
The operation was carried out by personnel from the Police Intervention and Surveillance Squadron of the 2nd Division and the Prevention and Immediate Reaction Teams (EPRI), says the PSP
According to the PSP, the detainee is now before the Judicial Authorities.
