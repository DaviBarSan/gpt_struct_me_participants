Lisbon, 30 November 2020
CPR // ROC
Four men detained by PSP after shooting in Lisbon
Four men, in two different vehicles, were arrested today after shooting at each other with firearms in the Rego area, in Lisbon, with no injuries, only damage to vehicles parked in the area, said a police source.
Speaking to Lusa, a source from the PSP Lisbon Metropolitan Command explained that the incident happened around 12:00.
The four men, of an unspecified age, fled after the shooting, but ended up intercepted by authorities and detained.
“There are no injuries to report, just damage to vehicles that were at the scene”, said the same source, noting that “several bullet casings” were left at the scene.
According to the same source, one of the suspects had a magazine with several ammunition in his possession when he was detained.
The case was handed over to the Judiciary Police.
