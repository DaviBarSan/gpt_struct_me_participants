Vila Nova de Gaia, Porto, 15 Dec 2020
JGJ // ACG
Man shot in Vila Nova de Gaia and shooter fled
A 31-year-old man was shot late this morning in Gulpilhares, Vila Nova de Gaia, and the shooter fled, a police source said.
The victim was hit in the left arm and was taken to the Santos Silva hospital, in Vila de Gaia, district of Porto, by firefighters from Valadares, according to a source from this corporation.
This is a slightly injured person.
The alert reached the authorities at 11:45 am and the Judiciary Police were called to investigate, as this is a case within the reserved jurisdiction of that criminal police body.
The reasons for the crime are yet to be determined.
