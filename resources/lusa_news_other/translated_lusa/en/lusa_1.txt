VAM // JH
Covid-19: GNR ended illegal party with 50 people in São Brás de Alportel
On Saturday, the GNR ended an illegal party in a bar in Corotelo, in the municipality of São Brás de Alportel, which brought together around 50 people, it was announced today.
In a statement, the Faro Territorial Command of the GNR said that, following a complaint reporting the existence of “a party with live music in a drinking establishment”, soldiers from the São de Alportel Territorial Post went to the location to monitor the incident.
The military demobilized people to “compliance with the rules relating to the covid-19 pandemic”.
The note also states that the owner was identified and “an administrative offense notice” was drawn up.
