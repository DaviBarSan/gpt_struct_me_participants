Braga, 05 Nov 2020
VCP // JAP
PJ arrested couple suspected of killing a woman in Braga to take her house
The Judiciary Police (PJ) detained a couple suspected of killing a 69-year-old woman in Braga, to obtain and appropriate the victim's assets, a source from that force told Lusa today.
According to the source, the male member of the couple had a romantic relationship with the victim.
“The objective of the crime was to seize the assets that the victim had with him and also the house that belonged to him,” he added.
The victim, aged 69, was murdered, in an urban parish of Braga, “through the use of physical force” and the body was transported by the suspects to Dume, in the same municipality, where it was abandoned on a path.
The detainees, he is 61 years old and she is 48 years old, live in the city of Braga.
They are accused of crimes of qualified homicide, desecration of a corpse, computer fraud and theft.
They will be presented to court for the first judicial interrogation and application of the respective coercive measures.
