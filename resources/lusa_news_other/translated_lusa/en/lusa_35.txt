Idanha-a-Nova, Castelo Branco, October 23, 2020
CCC // JEF
GNR arrests man for drug cultivation and trafficking in Idanha-a-Nova
The GNR arrested, in flagrante delicto, a 52-year-old man for cultivation and drug trafficking in Idanha-a-Nova, in the district of Castelo Branco, the security force announced today.
In a statement, the Castelo Branco Territorial Command explains that the arrest of the 52-year-old man was carried out on Thursday, through the Criminal Investigation Center (NIC) ofÂ Idanha-a-Nova.
"As part of a policing action, the military detected the suspect on agricultural land cultivating cannabis, and he was arrested at the time", the note reads.
The military also seized six kilos of cannabis heads, 2.25 kilos of marijuana, 26 cannabis plants, a scale and a mill.
This action was reinforced by soldiers from the GNR Territorial Post of Idanha-a-Nova
The detainee will be brought before the Idanha-a-Nova Judicial Court.
