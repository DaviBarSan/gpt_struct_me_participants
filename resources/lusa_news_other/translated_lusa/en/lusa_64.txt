Bissau, October 26, 2020
MSE // VM
Guinea-Bissau PJ detains man with three kilos of cocaine at the airport
The Judicial Police of Guinea-Bissau arrested a man in possession of three kilograms of cocaine on Saturday when he tried to board a flight to Lisbon, the police force announced in a statement.
According to the statement, the man, a Portuguese-Guinean citizen, was carrying the drug concealed on his waist and was detained at Osvaldo Vieira international airport, in Bissau.
The PJ adds that the man, aged 47, will appear before the Public Prosecutor's Office today.
At the beginning of October, the PJ had also detained another Portuguese-Guinean citizen, aged 22, at Osvaldo Vieira airport, in possession of a kilogram of cocaine, when he was preparing to travel to Lisbon.
As part of that arrest, the PJ also detained three men for alleged involvement in the "acquisition, delivery and facilitation of the introduction" of drugs inside the airport.
