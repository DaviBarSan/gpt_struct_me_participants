Vila Real, November 24, 2020
PLI // ACG
GNR found an elderly woman reported missing in the village of Vila Real
This afternoon, the GNR found the 81-year-old woman who had been missing since Monday in Sabroso, in the municipality of Vila Real, a source from that police force said today.
The octogenarian was detected by a dog from the GNR dog team about two kilometers from her residence and was transported to the Vila Real unit of the Tr√°s-os-Montes and Alto Douro Hospital Center.
The alert for the disappearance of the elderly woman, who lives with a family member, was given at 5pm on Monday, having been searched for by members of the GNR's Emergency Protection and Relief Unit (UEPS) during the night.
The searches were resumed this morning, involving members of the GNR and firefighters, and the woman was found at 3:40 pm.
According to the website of the National Emergency and Civil Protection Authority, 29 officers and nine vehicles were mobilized for this incident.
