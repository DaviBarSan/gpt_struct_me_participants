GNR seizes 785 kilos of hashish and detains two men on a boat off Olhão
The GNR Coastal Control Unit (UCC) seized 785 kilos of hashish on Sunday and detained two men when they were transshipping the narcotic product between two vessels off Olhão (Algarve), it was announced today.
In a statement, the National Republican Guard (GNR) specified that the two vessels were detected through the Integrated Surveillance and Command and Control System (SIVICC) “coasted while transshipping objects that resembled bales of hashish”.
After the UCC maritime resources were called, “the vessel that was receiving the narcotic product was intercepted while still at sea”, and the two crew members, aged 24 and 26, of the fiber vessel, measuring around five meters in length, were arrested. length, indicated the GNR.
On board the vessel were 22 bales of hashish, with a total weight of 785 kilos and an estimated value of more than two million euros.
