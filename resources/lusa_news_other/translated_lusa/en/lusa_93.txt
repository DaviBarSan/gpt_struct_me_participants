Man found dead in the Tua River in the Mirandela area
A 32-year-old man was found dead early this morning in the Tua River, in the Valverde da Gestosa area, in Mirandela, where he had gone fishing, reported the GNR.
According to information provided to Lusa by that security force, the man left home on Sunday morning to go fishing.
At around 7:30 pm, authorities were alerted to the disappearance and began searching the area.
The body was found after midnight and removed from the river near the place where authorities found the fisherman's belongings.
According to the source, the case was handed over to the Judiciary Police to investigate the circumstances of the death.
