Felgueiras, Porto, 18 December 2020
APM // JLS
Man seriously injured while cutting tree in Felgueiras
A 31-year-old man was seriously injured today when he was cutting a tree, in Regilde, in the municipality of Felgueiras, and fell into a ravine measuring around seven meters, a fire source told Lusa.
The victim was hit by the falling tree at around 5:55 pm, ending up thrown into the ravine, adds the source from the Felgueiras corporation.
According to commander Júlio Pereira, the victim is a firefighter in Vizela and had to be rescued by the corporation's wide-angle team.
The Felgueiras Fire Department moved an ambulance, the extrication vehicle and the high-angle rescue vehicle to the scene.
The man was also assisted by the Emergency and Resuscitation Medical Vehicle (VMER) team from Vale do Sousa and transported to Guimarães Hospital.
The GNR was at the scene and took charge of the incident.
