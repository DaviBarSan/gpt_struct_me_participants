Óbidos, Leiria, 24 December 2020
TDI // MCL
Man detained in Óbidos for hunting near houses
A 50-year-old man was detained by the National Republican Guard (GNR), on Wednesday, for hunting near houses, that force of authority announced today.
“Following a complaint, and after investigative measures, the military intercepted the suspect hunting in land close to houses, in a protective strip of less than 250 meters”, said the GNR statement, which mobilized the Óbidos Territorial Post and the Caldas da Rainha Environmental Protection Center.
The man was accused of the crime of hunting on unauthorized land and the report of the situation was forwarded to the Caldas da Rainha Court.
The arrest also resulted in the seizure of a hunting rifle, two 12-gauge cartridges and various materials and accessories related to hunting.
