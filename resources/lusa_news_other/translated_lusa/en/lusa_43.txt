GNR arrests man for mistreating and threatening to kill his 73-year-old mother in Gondomar
A 50-year-old man was arrested today for physically and psychologically abusing his 73-year-old mother, in Gondomar, in the Porto district, the GNR said.
Following a report that a crime of domestic violence was taking place, the military went to the scene and discovered that the suspect was inflicting physical and psychological abuse, as well as death threats using sharp weapons on the mother. , this police force highlighted in a statement.
After carrying out a home search, the GNR seized two bladed weapons, he stressed.
The suspect, with a criminal record for committing the same crimes, was arrested and will undergo his first interrogation at the Porto Judicial Court, on Saturday.
