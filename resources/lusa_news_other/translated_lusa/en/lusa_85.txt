Marvão, Portalegre, November 19, 2020
TCA // RBF
61-year-old man dies in car crash in Marvão
A 61-year-old man died today following the crash of the car he was driving on Municipal Road 524 in the municipality of Marvão, district of Portalegre, Civil Protection and GNR sources said.
The same sources told the Lusa agency that the death was declared at the scene by the doctor in the emergency and resuscitation medical vehicle (VMER), at the Portalegre hospital, and that the man was the only occupant of the passenger vehicle.
According to a source from the District Relief Operations Command (CDOS) of Portalegre, the accident occurred in the Bica area, in the parish of Beirã, and the man's body was sent to the Forensic Medicine service at the Portalegre hospital.
The alert, according to CDOS, was given at 7:23 pm.
Firefighters and vehicles from the Marvão corporation and VMER were mobilized, in addition to the GNR, in a total of eight elements, supported by four vehicles.
