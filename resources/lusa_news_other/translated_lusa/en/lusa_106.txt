Truck crane knocked down lighting pole in Porto and killed 52-year-old man
A 52-year-old man died today, and another suffered minor injuries, following an accident with a truck that knocked down an electricity pole on Rua da Constitution, in Porto, a PSP source told Lusa.
According to the Public Relations source of the Porto PSP Metropolitan Command, the lighting post was knocked down by the truck's crane, hitting the man who was walking on the street.
