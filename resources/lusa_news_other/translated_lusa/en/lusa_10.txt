Car runs over several people during "Black Lives Matter" demonstration in Manhattan
A car driver ran over several people today during a demonstration of the “Black Lives Matter” movement, in Manhattan, New York, in the United States, but authorities have not yet specified whether it was an accident or intentional action.
The incident caused several injuries, some of whom had to be transported to various hospitals in the 'Big Apple', the New York police (NYPD) explained to France-Presse (AFP).
The driver of the car was detained at the scene, according to the same source.
The police spokesperson said that the incident, which occurred at 4pm local time (9pm in Lisbon) has not resulted in any deaths so far.
Several fire brigades and ambulances are at the scene.
