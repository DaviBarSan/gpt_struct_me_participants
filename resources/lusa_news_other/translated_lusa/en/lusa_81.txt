Paredes, Porto, 15 November 2020
JFO//JPS
Man accused in Paredes for possession of prohibited weapons
A 25-year-old man, with a criminal record, was arrested in Paredes by the GNR and charged with having in his possession a replica firearm and an extendable baton, the Guard revealed today in a statement.
The arrest, made by the Criminal Investigation Center (NIC) of Penafiel, was made on Friday, as part of an investigation into suspected possession of a prohibited weapon that lasted around a month, the document also reads. .
Also according to the GNR, the military carried out "two home searches and one non-home search", which resulted in the "seizure of a replica firearm and an extendable baton".
The detainee, adds the Guard, has "a criminal record for driving without a legal license and threats" and was "indicted" and the facts were sent to the Paredes Judicial Court.
