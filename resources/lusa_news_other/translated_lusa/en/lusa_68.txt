Santa Marta de Penaguião, Vila Real, October 27, 2020
PLI // ACG
84-year-old man dies after being run over in Santa Marta de Penaguião
An 84-year-old man died today after being run over in Vila Maior, municipality of Santa Marta de Penaguião, district of Vila Real, said a GNR source.
According to the Santa Marta de Penaguião fire commander, Vasco Correia, death was declared at the scene by the Emergency and Resuscitation Medical Vehicle (VMER) team.
The accident, according to the GNR, occurred during a reversing maneuver by a small car.
The alert for the collision was given at 11:29 am and six firefighters from Santa Marta de Penaguião, GNR and INEM were mobilized to the scene, with the support of three vehicles.
