Barcelos, Braga, 24 Oct 2020
CCM // TDI
48-year-old man dies in car collision in Barcelos
A 48-year-old man died today, the victim of a collision between two light vehicles in the parish of Chorente, municipality of Barcelos (Braga), said a source from the District Relief Operations Command (CDOS) of Braga.
The accident between the two cars was recorded at around 1 pm.
The fatal victim was the driver of one of the vehicles, and a slightly injured person was also reported, around 20 years old, who was transported to Barcelos Hospital, said a source from the Braga CDOS and the Viatodos Volunteer Fire Department.
