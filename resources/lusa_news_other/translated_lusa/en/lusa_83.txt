Setúbal, 16 November 2020
TCA//JLS
Man dies after moped he was driving is hit by a light vehicle in Setúbal
A 34-year-old man died today after the moped he was driving was hit by a light vehicle on Estrada Nacional 10-8, in the Mitrena area, in Setúbal, sources from the PSP and Civil Protection said.
A source from the District Relief Operations Command (CDOS) of Setúbal told the Lusa agency that the man's death was declared at the scene, and the body was sent to the Forensic Medicine service at Hospital de São Bernardo, in Setúbal.
The alert, according to the CDOS, was given at 06:55, with firefighters and vehicles from the Palmela and Sapadores de Setúbal corporations and an emergency and resuscitation medical vehicle (VMER) being mobilized, in addition to the PSP, in a total of 16 elements, supported by five vehicles.
