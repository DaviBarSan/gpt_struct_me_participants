Aveiro, 10 November 2020
JFO // ACG
GNR seizes 390 kilograms of bluefin tuna in Aveiro
The GNR seized 390 kilograms of bluefin tuna, the capture of which is prohibited and could lead to a fine of up to 37,500 euros, in the coastal fishing port of Gafanha da Nazar√©, in Aveiro, the Guard revealed today in a statement.
The seizure occurred on Monday during an inspection with the aim of controlling compliance with the rules for unloading, transport, marketing and the first sale regime for fresh fish, according to the press release.
During the operation, the military found "77 kilos of bluefin tuna" in a vehicle, and following further investigations ended up locating "the fishing vessel responsible, still having 313 kilos of bluefin tuna inside", adds the GNR.
Also according to the Guard, "the driver of the vehicle and the master of the vessel were identified" and the "report of administrative offense was drawn up due to the fact that the transport and capture of this species is prohibited, an infraction punishable by a fine of up to 37,500 euros".
"The seized fish, after being subject to hygienic control, was considered fit for consumption and donated to a social solidarity institution", adds the statement.
