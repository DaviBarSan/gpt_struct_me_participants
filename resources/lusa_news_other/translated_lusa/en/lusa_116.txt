Two injured in fight at Casal dos Machados in Lisbon
Two men aged 22 and 33 were injured, one of them seriously, on Tuesday night following a disorder in the Casal dos Machados neighborhood, in Lisbon, a PSP source told Lusa.
According to the source, the disorder, which involved several people and firearms, began near the gas pumps on Avenida Infante D. Henrique and ended in Largo Maria Judite de Carvalho, in Casal dos Machados, in the parish of Parque das Nations.
“Following the disorder, a 33-year-old man was seriously injured and was transported by ambulance to Santa Maria Hospital. The slightly injured person, aged 22, went to the same hospital by his own means”, said the PSP source.
According to the PSP, a firearm cartridge was found at the scene.
“Three vehicles were also damaged due to gunshots,” he said.
The alert for disorder was given at 9:49 pm.
The investigation was carried out by the Judicial Police.
