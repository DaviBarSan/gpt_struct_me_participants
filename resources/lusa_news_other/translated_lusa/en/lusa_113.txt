Vila Nova de Foz Côa, Guarda, 28 December 2020
FYP // ACG
Domestic violence: Preventive arrest for 26-year-old man in Foz Côa
A 26-year-old man was detained in pre-trial detention for an alleged crime of domestic violence on Christmas Day, due to death threats to his mother and sister, in the municipality of Foz Côa, the GNR announced today.
"Following a tip, the Guard soldiers went to the location on Christmas Day where they found that the suspect had insulted and threatened to kill, using an ax and a knife, the 46-year-old mother and sister of 12 years", described that security force.
According to the GNR, the attacker also destroyed the doors of the house, in order to intimidate the victims.
"In the presence of the military, he tried to escape, having been intercepted and detained. The objects used for the threats were seized", indicates the Guard, in a statement.
The detainee, with a criminal record for offenses of the same nature, was present for the first interrogation at the Judicial Court of Seia, being subject to the coercive measure of preventive detention.
