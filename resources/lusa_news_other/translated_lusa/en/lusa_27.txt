Lisbon, October 11, 2020
VAM // JH
Child injured in accident on the North highway died in hospital - GNR
The 12-year-old child who had been seriously injured in an accident that occurred on the highway in the North in the area of ​​São João da Talha, Loures, on Saturday night, ended up dying in hospital, a GNR source told Lusa.
The accident, which occurred at around 9:40 pm on Saturday, at kilometer four of the North Motorway, in the North/South direction, involved three cars and caused four other injuries, all not serious.
Previously, a source from the GNR General Command had told Lusa that the 12-year-old child had been trapped in one of the vehicles involved in the accident and was subsequently transported to the hospital.
Traffic was closed, but by 11:30 pm on Saturday it was already circulating “on the side lane”.
There were 22 firefighters, the National Institute of Medical Emergency (INEM) and the police at the scene of the accident, supported by 10 vehicles.
