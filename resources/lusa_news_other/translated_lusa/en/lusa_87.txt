Suspicion of arson in a fire that consumed an uninhabited house in Câmara de Lobos
A fire in an uninhabited house in the Jardim da Serra parish, in the municipality of Câmara de Lobos, completely damaged the house, with arson being suspected, a local authority source said today.
“It was a house that was not inhabited and there is suspicion of arson,” said the councilor of Câmara de Lobos, Leonel Correia da Silva, to the Lusa agency.
The mayor mentioned that the house “had no maintenance and was completely uninhabitable”, adding that the “damage caused by the fire is only material”.
Câmara de Lobos firefighters fought the fire.
