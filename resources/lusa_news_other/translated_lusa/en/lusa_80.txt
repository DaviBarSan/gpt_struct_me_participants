Estarreja, Aveiro, 15 November 2020
JFO//JPS
Man found dead in truck tank in Estarreja
A 34-year-old man, an employee of Transportadora J Amaral, in Estarreja, was found dead today inside the tank of a truck, a GNR source from Aveiro told Lusa.
The alert was given at 9:56 am and the man, who was allegedly carrying out maintenance on the cistern, had been missing since Saturday, the Estarreja Fire Commander, Joaquim Rebelo, added to Lusa.
The cistern where the transport employee was working was open, added the graduate, who was unable to determine the cause of death.
Also according to the GNR source, the Health Delegate and the Judiciary Police arrived at the scene, who took charge of the incident.
The Aveiro Emergency and Resuscitation Medical Vehicle was also at the scene, which transported the body to Hospital Infante D. Pedro, added the fire department source.
