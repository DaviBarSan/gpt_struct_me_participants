Lisbon, 06 Nov 2020
PMF // JPS
Government: Ricardo Pinheiro replaces José Mendes as Secretary of State for Planning
Socialist deputy Ricardo Pinheiro will replace José Mendes in the position of Secretary of State for Planning, a change that was announced today through a note published on the Presidency of the Republic portal.
"The President of the Republic accepted the Prime Minister's proposal to dismiss, at his request, José Fernandes Gomes Mendes, as Secretary of State for Planning, and the appointment, to the same position, of Ricardo Miguel Furtado Pinheiro", reads- if in the note.
The inauguration of the new Secretary of State of the Minister of Planning, Nelson de Sousa, will take place today, at 8:30 pm, at the Palácio de Belém.
Ricardo Pinheiro was president of the Chamber of Campo Maior, district of Portalegre, a circle for which he was elected deputy in the last legislative elections.
