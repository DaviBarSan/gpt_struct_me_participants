Mértola, Beja, 15 Dec 2020
LL // ROC
GNR suspects drug trafficking and seizes 12,300 doses of hashish in Mértola
The GNR detained a 36-year-old woman on suspicion of committing the crime of drug trafficking and seized 12,300 doses of hashish in the Alentejo municipality of Mértola, in the district of Beja, it was announced today.
The woman was detained by military personnel from the Mértola Territorial Post on Monday and is today present at the Beja Judicial Court for the first interrogation and possible application of coercive measures, says the GNR, in a statement.
According to the security force, during a patrol action on Estrada Nacional 122, near the town of Mértola, the suspect, who was traveling in a vehicle, "demonstrated nervous behavior", after being approached by GNR soldiers.
The military searched the vehicle and searched the woman, which allowed them to determine that she had drugs in her possession and seize the 12,300 doses of hashish, the vehicle and a cell phone.
