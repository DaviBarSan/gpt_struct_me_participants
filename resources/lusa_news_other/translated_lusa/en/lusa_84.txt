Barcelos, Braga, 17 November 2020
VCP // JAP
Man dies after being hit by a large tree in Barcelos
A man aged around 50 died today in Milhazes, in the municipality of Barcelos, hit by a large tree, said a source from the Braga District Relief Operations Command.
According to the source, the circumstances of the accident have not yet been determined, but the first information points to a work accident.
“According to initial information, the man was cutting the tree and was hit by it,” he said.
The alert was given at 11:50, and the Barcelinhos Volunteer Firefighters and an emergency and resuscitation medical vehicle were mobilized to the scene.
Death was declared at the scene.
The GNR took charge of the incident.
