Man suffers second and third degree burns in Famalicão
A man today suffered burns “on his face and upper limbs” while handling gasoline in Vale de S. Martinho, Vila Nova de Famalicão, and had to be taken to Hospital de São João, Porto, according to a local fire department source.
The 58-year-old man suffered second and third degree burns and the alert was given at 1:41 pm.
A source from the Famalicenses Volunteer Firefighters told Lusa that “the victim was taken to Hospital de São João” and that “the cause of the accident is still unknown”, knowing “only that it is related to the handling of gasoline”.
Transport was carried out by the Emergency and Resuscitation Medical Vehicle (VMER) from Famalicão Hospital, in the district of Braga.
