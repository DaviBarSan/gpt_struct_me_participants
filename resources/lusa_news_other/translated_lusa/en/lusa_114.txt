Évora, 30 Oct 2020
SYM//ROC
Judicial Police arrest suspect of child pornography in Évora
The Judiciary Police (PJ) today announced the arrest, in flagrante delicto, of a 56-year-old man, resident in the district of Évora, on suspicion of committing the crime of pornography of minors via cell phones.
In a statement, the PJ indicated that the suspect was located and detained by members of the Évora Local Criminal Investigation Unit.
The investigation arose following information collected by the PJ, said the police, adding that, after a home search, "several cell phones" were seized from the man, which contained "more than two hundred computer files".
According to the PJ, the suspect used this equipment to share content with "images of minors in sexual situations".
The detainee, already referenced for the same type of crime, will be present for the first judicial interrogation for the possible application of coercive measures.
