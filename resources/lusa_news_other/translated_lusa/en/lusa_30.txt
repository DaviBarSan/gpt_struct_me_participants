GNR arrests two men for drug trafficking in Sines
The GNR arrested two men, aged between 34 and 43, for drug trafficking, in Sines, in the district of Setúbal, having seized 1,673 doses of drugs, the security force announced today.
In a statement, the National Republican Guard (GNR) indicated that the arrest of the two suspects was carried out on Saturday, during a patrol in a location already known for drug trafficking, in the municipality of Sines.
“After verifying suspicious movements, in a location already known for drug trafficking, the military detained the two suspects when they were preparing to carry out a transaction”, said the Setúbal territorial command of the GNR.
During the operation, 1,570 doses of heroin, 103 doses of cocaine, 844 euros in cash, a vehicle and four cell phones were seized.
The detainees will be presented to the Santiago do Cacém Judicial Court for the first judicial interrogation and application of possible coercive measures.
