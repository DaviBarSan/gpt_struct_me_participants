Vila Nova de Gaia, Porto, 15 November 2020
JFO//JPS
Body of man found floating in the Douro River, in Vila Nova de Gaia
The corpse of a man aged around 60 was found floating in the Douro River, in Vila Nova de Gaia, today, having been removed from the water by the Maritime Police, the commander of the Porto captaincy confirmed to Lusa.
According to Rui Santos Amaral, the maritime authority was “alerted by members of the public” who saw the “body of a man floating next to the Santa Marinha floating pier”, in Vila Nova de Gaia, at around 5:20 pm.
The Maritime Police then removed the body from the water, and then waited for the arrival of the Judiciary Police to begin investigations.
The Sapadores from Vila Nova de Gaia and the Firefighters from Coimbrões were also present at the scene.
