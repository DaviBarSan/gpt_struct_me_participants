Monção, Viana do Castelo, October 23, 2020
ABC // JAP
Man dies hit by tree in Monção
A 59-year-old man died today after being hit by a tree while felling it, in Barreiro, parish of Tangil, in Monção, district of Viana do Castelo, said a civil protection source.
According to a source from the District Relief Operations Command (CDOS) of Viana do Castelo, the death was declared at the scene by the helicopter doctor from the National Institute of Medical Emergency (INEM), called to the scene.
The source said that the man "was doing forestry work when the tree he was cutting fell on him".
The alert was given at 2:25 pm.
An INEM source contacted by Lusa explained that, in addition to the air transport, two ambulances were called, from Monção and Valença, as initial information pointed to the existence of two injured people, which was not proven.
INEM called a team of psychologists to the scene.
A total of 16 firefighters from the Monção and Valença Fire Department and five vehicles attended the scene, including the Immediate Life Support (SIV) ambulance from Melgaço.
