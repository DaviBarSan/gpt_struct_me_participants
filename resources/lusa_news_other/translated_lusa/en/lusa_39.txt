Santa Maria da Feira, Aveiro, 11 November 2020
JGJ // ACG
GNR is looking for one of those involved in the robbery of the Feira confectionery
Two men robbed the cash register of a confectionery shop in the municipality of Santa Maria da Feira today and one of the two involved is on the run from the authorities, said an official GNR source.
The robbery was carried out between 12:00 and 12:30 in a confectionery shop in Milheirós de Poiares, Santa Maria da Feria, district of Aveiro, and those involved acted with their faces covered.
They took the cash register with an amount of money that has not yet been determined, according to the source.
One of the robbers was still detained in Milheirós de Poiares and the other was still being sought at around 2:30 pm.
In the search for the man “still at large”, resources from the territorial device of the GNR and the Intervention Detachment of that police force are involved.
