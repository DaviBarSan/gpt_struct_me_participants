Amadora, Lisbon, 22 December 2020
JML // ROC
Young man is in very serious condition after being shot in the chest in Amadora
A 17-year-old young man was in a very serious condition after being shot in the chest this morning in the Casal da Mira neighborhood, in Amadora, in the Lisbon district, a PSP source told the Lusa agency.
According to a source from the Lisbon Metropolitan Command (Cometlis), the alert for the occurrence was given at 00:53.
“A 17-year-old boy was shot with a firearm. He was injured in a very serious condition and was transported to the Hospital Professor Doutor Fernando Fonseca [Amadora-Sintra]”, he said.
To Lusa, Cometlis said that the causes of the incident are unknown and that no suspects have yet been identified or detained.
As it was a crime, the case was handed over to the Judiciary Police (PJ) for investigation.
