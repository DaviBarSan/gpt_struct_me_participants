60-year-old man dies in Matosinhos after falling from roof he was cleaning
A 60-year-old man died today, in Matosinhos, in the district of Porto, after falling from the roof of his house, which he was cleaning, a PSP source told Lusa
The accident occurred at around 3:30 pm, on Rua Augusto Gil, with the man falling from a height of seven meters, the source added.
