Espinho, Aveiro, 28 December 2020
JDN // ACG
Man arrested on suspicion of arson in building in Espinho
A 54-year-old man was arrested by the PSP on suspicion of having set fire to a residential building in Espinho, in the district of Aveiro, which caused 16 injuries due to smoke inhalation, that police force reported today.
In a statement, the PSP clarifies that the alleged perpetrator of the fire, resident there, was inside the building, and at the time of his interception confirmed that he had placed some of his clothes in the entrance hall of the said building, setting them on fire. .
According to the PSP, 16 people received medical assistance on site due to smoke inhalation, however, there was no need for transport to any hospital unit.
"There was no significant material damage due to the fire, meaning the building remained habitable, with no need for relocation", states the same note.
The fire, which broke out at 7:55 pm on Saturday, was extinguished at 8:20 pm by the Espinho Volunteer Firefighters.
The PSP informed the Judicial Police of this case, which will continue with the respective investigation.
