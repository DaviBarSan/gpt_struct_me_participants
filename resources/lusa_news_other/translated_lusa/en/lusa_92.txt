Vila Nova de Famalicão, Braga, November 27, 2020
VCP // MSP
Man died after falling from a roof where he worked in Famalicão
A man died today in Jesufrei, Vila Nova de Famalicão, after falling from a roof, said a source from the Braga District Relief Operations Command.
According to the source, the victim was between 60 and 65 years old and was working on the roof.
He will have suffered a fall of around eight meters in height.
The alert was given at 3:30 pm, and death was declared at the scene.
For assistance, an emergency and resuscitation medical vehicle (VMER) was mobilized, in addition to firefighters, totaling six men and three vehicles.
The GNR took charge of the incident.
