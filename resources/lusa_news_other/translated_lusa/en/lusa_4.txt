Penafiel, Porto, 14 December 2020
APM // ACG
Domestic violence: Electronic bracelet for man detained in Penafiel
A 39-year-old man was placed under electronic surveillance, in Penafiel, Porto district, suspected of physical violence, insults and death threats to his 42-year-old ex-partner, a GNR source said today.
In a statement, the police authority indicated that the suspect "did not accept the separation and began chasing the victim at her home and workplace."
The woman was already using a panic button, which had been applied to her as a teleassistance protection measure.
The man, with a criminal record for offenses of the same nature, was also prohibited from contacting the victim.
