Portalegre, 09 Nov 2020
TCA // MCL
61-year-old man found dead inside car in Portalegre
A 61-year-old man was found dead this morning inside his car in Carreiras, in the municipality of Portalegre, a GNR source told Lusa.
According to the same source, the alert to the GNR was given at around 7:00 am, after the man was seen by members of the public, inside his vehicle, allegedly dead.
The GNR soldiers who went to the scene found that the man had injuries to his face, and the intervention of the Judiciary Police, which is investigating the case, was requested, said the security force source.
According to the guard source, the man, born and residing in Carreiras, lived alone and had been seen alive in that location on Sunday, between 8:00 pm and 9:30 pm.
According to a source from the District Relief Operations Command (CDOS) of Portalegre, the death was declared at the scene, and the body was sent to the Forensic Medicine service at the Portalegre hospital.
