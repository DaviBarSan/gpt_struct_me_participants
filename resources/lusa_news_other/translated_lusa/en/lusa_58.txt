Angolan government regrets death of two people in hospital fire in Cunene
The Minister of Health of Angola, Sílvia Lutucuta, lamented the deaths of two people, following the fire recorded at the General Hospital in the province of Cunene.
A note from the Ministry of Health informs that the minister, who traveled to that southern province of Angola on Tuesday, to check the damage caused by the fire at the General Hospital of Ondjiva, capital of Cunene, lamented the death of a seriously ill patient and a newborn, who needed special care.
The fire recorded on Monday night affected the operating room areas, surgical wards and the oxygen storage station.
Sílvia Lutucuta said that there was a need to transfer patients to other hospital units in the area, highlighting that a frontline team mobilized to avoid the worst with the other patients.
All patients were transferred to various health centers and for better care they will be placed in the new Municipal Hospital of Cuanhama, which is in the final stages of construction.
