Editorial, December 22, 2020
JGJ // ACG
GNR arrests four suspects of robberies and drug trafficking in Alto Minho
The GNR today arrested four men allegedly involved in robberies of residences and establishments, thefts from inside vehicles and drug trafficking in Alto Minho, announced the Viana do Castelo command of that police force.
The suspects, aged between 35 and 41, all with criminal records, were arrested as part of an investigation carried out over half a year into several thefts in the municipalities of Arcos de Valdevez, Paredes de Coura, Ponte da Barca and Ponte de Lima, all in the district of Viana do Castelo.
They also allegedly committed some crimes in Vila Verde, in the district of Braga.
The operation now announced included house searches that resulted in the seizure of heroin for 56 doses, cocaine for 17 and hashish for eight, in addition to six cell phones, two computers, a camera and a GPS device.
