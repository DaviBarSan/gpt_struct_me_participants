Lisbon, 09 Dec 2020
JML //MCL
Man dies after being stabbed in the back near the Arroios market in Lisbon
A 29-year-old man died today after being stabbed in the back near the Arroios market, in Lisbon, following a disagreement, the PSP informed the Lusa agency.
According to the Lisbon Metropolitan Command (Cometlis), the stabbing took place on Rua Ângela Pinto, at 10:54, and the death was declared inside a cafe, where the victim went to ask for help.
“It may have been a disagreement between two individuals that involved a sharp weapon, with one of them being stabbed in the back,” said a Cometlis source.
Still collecting information about the case, the PSP said that the suspect in the crime was arrested on Avenida Almirante Reis after attempting to escape. The police said they are awaiting more information regarding the suspect.
A police source also told Lusa that the Judiciary Police (PJ) had already been called because it was a crime of homicide.
At around 2:34 pm, the crime scene was preserved by police forces and with security tapes.
