Man seriously injured after being electrocuted in Oleiros
A 66-year-old man was seriously injured this afternoon after being electrocuted, in Oleiros, in the district of Castelo Branco, a source from the District Relief Operations Command told the Lusa agency.
According to the same source, the alert was given at 4:18 pm, and the victim was transported directly to the Coimbra Hospital and University Center.
"According to firefighters, the electrocution took place inside a house," he added.
Four operatives and two vehicles went to the scene.
