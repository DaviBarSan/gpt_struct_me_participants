Vila Nova de Gaia, Porto, October 18, 2020
JFO // JNM
25-year-old man suffers third-degree burns to limbs after house fire
A 25-year-old man suffered third-degree burns on his upper and lower limbs today following a fire at his house, in Arcozelo, Vila Nova de Gaia, a local Sapadores source told Lusa.
The alert was given at 8:37 am and the Vila Nova de Gaia Firefighters and Aguda Firefighters were fighting the flames that started in the kitchen of the house, the source added.
Assisted at the scene, the man was then taken to the Vila Nova de Gaia/Espinho Hospital Center, he said.
The fire that partially consumed the house was extinguished at 8:51 am, Civil Protection reported.
