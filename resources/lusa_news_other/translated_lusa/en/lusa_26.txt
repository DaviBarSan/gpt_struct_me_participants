Editorial, January 4, 2021
VAYA // NFO
Alex Apolinário in serious condition after cardiorespiratory arrest
Brazilian footballer Alex Apolinário, from Alverca, is in serious condition and in an induced coma, after suffering a cardiorespiratory arrest on Sunday, the Portuguese Championship club reported today.
"According to information from the clinical staff at Vila Franca de Xira Hospital, the athlete is in a serious condition, stabilized and in an induced coma", reads a note published on the club's Facebook.
In the same statement, Ribatejo residents thank "the wave of solidarity with the athlete who arrived from the four corners of the world".
"On behalf of Alverca Futebol SAD, on behalf of the athlete and his family, we thank the health professionals, and everyone who sent messages, clubs, athletes, institutions and fans from all over the world, who joined us in a gigantic demonstration of appreciation for human life", concluded the statement from SAD Alverquense.
On Sunday, Alex Apolinário suffered a cardiorespiratory arrest during the Alverca - União de Almeirim game, counting for the Portuguese Championship.
