Aveiro, 30 Oct 2020
JDN // JAP
GNR seized nine illegal nurseries in the Aveiro estuary
The GNR Coastal Control Unit (UCC) seized nine illegal nurseries in the Aveiro estuary with around 205 kilos of crustaceans and 20 kilos of European eel, valued at 2,765 euros, that military force reported today.
In a statement, the GNR clarifies that the fishponds were seized on Thursday, in a river patrol action carried out with the aim of controlling the rules for catching fish and maritime safety.
During this action, the Guard's soldiers detected nine illegal nurseries containing various crustaceans and 20 kilos of European eel, an endangered species that requires a certificate from the Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species of Wild Fauna and Flora. Extinction (CITES) for its detection.

The crustaceans and European eels, as they were alive, were returned to the natural environment.
