Man dies in disorder with firearm in Mem Martins in Sintra
One man died and another was injured on Sunday night in Algueirão-Mem Martins, in Sintra, in the Lisbon district, following a “disorder with a firearm”, the PSP informed the Lusa agency today.
According to the Lisbon Metropolitan Command (Cometlis), two people were found injured on Estrada de Mem Martins, one was transported to the Hospital Professor Doutor Fernando Fonseca (Amadora-Sintra) and the other was rescued at the scene, where he ended up dying .
“There was no way to reverse the injury situation,” said a Cometlis source.
Not knowing any further details, the PSP said that the alert was given at 11:30 pm and that, at the scene, only steps were taken to help the victims.
As it is a crime of homicide, the investigation of the case is the responsibility of the Judicial Police (PJ).
