Cartaxo, Santarém, 30 November 2020
AYR // ROC
Man dies in run over in Cartaxo
A road accident in Reguengo, Cartaxo, in the district of Santarém, left one dead and one seriously injured today, a source from the District Relief Operations Command told the Lusa agency.
According to the same source, the seriously injured person was transported to the Hospital de São José, in Lisbon, and the other victim was "declared a corpse at the scene", on Estrada Nacional 2-3, in the parish of Valada.
According to the Santarém GNR, the victims are two men, between 30 and 40 years old, who were walking and were run over by a light vehicle.
The alert was given at 6:14 pm, according to the website of the National Emergency and Civil Protection Authority, which reports that 13 operatives and five vehicles were on site two hours later.
