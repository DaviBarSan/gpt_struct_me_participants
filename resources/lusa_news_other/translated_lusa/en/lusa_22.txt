Lisbon, 17 December 2020
PMF // JPS
Covid-19: Costa in prophylactic isolation after being with Macron in Paris
The Portuguese Prime Minister is in preventive prophylactic isolation from today onwards, after being in Paris on Wednesday with the French President, Emmanuel Macron, who tested positive for the new coronavirus.
"Due to the confirmation of the positive test of French President Emmanuel Macron, with whom he was at the Élysée Palace yesterday, the Prime Minister is in preventive prophylactic isolation until the level of risk is assessed by the health authorities", it reads in the statement issued by António Costa's office.
Then, as the Lusa agency had already mentioned, it is said that, "as part of the preparation for the trip to São Tomé and Príncipe and Guinea Bissau, scheduled for December 18th to 20th, the prime minister carried out a test this morning for detection of Covid-19".
"The Prime Minister decided to cancel this trip, as well as any public agenda that involves his physical presence. He maintains all executive activity and his work agenda, which he will carry out remotely. The Prime Minister does not present any symptoms", he adds in the same statement.
