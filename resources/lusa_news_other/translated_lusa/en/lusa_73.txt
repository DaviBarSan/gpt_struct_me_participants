Ponte de Lima, Viana do Castelo, November 9, 2020
ABC // JAP
Man seriously injured after falling from scaffolding in Ponte de Lima
A 60-year-old man was in serious condition today after falling from a scaffold, about "three to four meters" high, while working on a construction site in Ponte de Lima, said the local fire commander.
Contacted by the Lusa agency, Carlos Lima explained that the man "was transported in serious condition, by the Immediate Life Support (SIV) ambulance, to the hospital in Braga".
According to the Fire Commander of Ponte de Lima, in the district of Viana do Castelo, "the work accident occurred at a construction site in the parish of Ribeira".
To Lusa, a source from the District Relief Operations Command (CDOS) said that the alert was given at around 10:33.
In addition to the SIV, two operatives and a fire engine, the Viana do Castelo Emergency Medical Vehicle (VMER) and the GNR, arrived at the scene.
