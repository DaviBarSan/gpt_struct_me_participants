Alcobaça, Leiria, 24 November 2020
DA //MCL
Man found dead in Alcobaça with signs of having been tied up
A 75-year-old man was found dead today, at home, with marks from having been tied to his feet and hands, in the town of Fervença, parish of Maiorga, in the municipality of Alcobaça, reported the GNR.
The GNR was alerted “by a family member who lives close to the victim”, a 75-year-old man who lived alone and who “was found dead, with signs of having been tied to his feet and mothers and signs that he had not been treated for a suicide,” Diogo Morgado, commander of the Caldas da Rainha Territorial Detachment of the GNR, told the Lusa agency.
According to the same person in charge, “the Judiciary Police were alerted, who analyzed the body and collected evidence at the scene”, the victim's residence, located in the district of Leiria.
The man's body was transported to the Legal Medicine service in Leiria, where it will be autopsied and the Judiciary Police are investigating the circumstances of his death.
Lusa contacted the Leiria Judiciary Police, but it has not yet been possible to obtain further clarification.
