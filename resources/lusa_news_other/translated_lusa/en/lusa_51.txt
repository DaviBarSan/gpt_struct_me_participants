ASR // JEF
GNR rescues 25 dogs victims of abuse in Seia
The GNR announced today that it had rescued 25 dogs and accused a 58-year-old man of mistreating pet animals, in the municipality of Seia, in the district of Guarda.
The Territorial Command of GNR da Guarda states in a statement that the animals were rescued during an action carried out on Tuesday through the Environmental Crimes and Administrative Offenses Investigation Center (NICCOA) and the Environmental Protection Center (NPA) of Gouveia.
“As part of an investigation into mistreatment of pet animals, the military carried out a home search warrant where they came across a scenario of overpopulation of dogs, without hygienic conditions, where the lack of care for the animals was visible. ”, the note reads.
The source adds that following police investigations, “25 dogs were rescued, all without electronic identification, without registration and without anti-rabies vaccination”.
The animals, which the owner welcomed inside his home, were handed over to the Seia Municipal Veterinarian, who collaborated in the action, according to the GNR.
The man was made a defendant and the facts were referred to the Seia Judicial Court.
