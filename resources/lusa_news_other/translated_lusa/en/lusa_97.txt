Barreiro, Setúbal, 04 Dec 2020
SYL // JLS
Man shot dead in Barreiro, shooter on the run
A man was shot dead today in Barreiro, shot with two shots from a shotgun and the shooter is on the run, a source from the National Directorate of the Public Security Police (PSP) told the Lusa agency.
The same source indicated that the incident occurred at 8:20 pm today, however, it did not provide further information about the circumstances of the shooting.
He only said that in the case of a suspected homicide involving firearms, the incident "is being monitored by the Judiciary Police”, responsible for investigating the case.
