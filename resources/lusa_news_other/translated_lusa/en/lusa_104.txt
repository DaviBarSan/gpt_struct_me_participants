Setúbal, 14 Dec 2020
GR // ROC
PJ arrests man suspected of shooting young girl in Setúbal community
The Judiciary Police (PJ) of Setúbal arrested a 63-year-old man suspected of having shot a young man, last Wednesday, in the restaurant of a community in Setúbal, it was announced today.
According to a statement from the PJ, the alleged attacker shot the victim following a conflict with a group of guests who were having lunch at the community, in Alto da Guerra.
The police say that the sixty-year-old ended up shooting a young man from the group with a firearm, causing considerable injuries to his shoulder and arm.
The detainee, who fled the scene, is charged with the crimes of attempted homicide, possession of a prohibited weapon and failure to provide assistance.
He was already present at his first judicial interrogation, and the coercive measure of preventive detention was applied to him.
