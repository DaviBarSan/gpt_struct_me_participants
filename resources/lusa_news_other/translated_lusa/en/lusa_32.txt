GNR seizes several types of firearms in action against domestic violence
The GNR seized a shotgun, a pistol, two compressed air guns and a holster during a home search, in Felgueiras, in the Porto district, as part of a complaint of domestic violence, it was announced today.
In a statement, the Guard explained that following a complaint related to a crime of domestic violence, the military "went to the location, where the victim, a 25-year-old woman, was the target of psychological coercion by her partner, a 33-year-old man ".
As part of the home search carried out on Monday, a 12-gauge shotgun, a 6.35mm caliber pistol, two compressed air guns, 64 rounds of ammunition and a holster were seized, the press release describes.
Also according to the GNR, the facts were sent to the Felgueiras Judicial Court.
