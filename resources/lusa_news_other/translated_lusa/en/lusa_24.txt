Castro Marim, December 13, 2020
MICA // ANP
Authorities search to find missing man in Guadiana River
The National Maritime Authority today carried out searches, so far without success, on the Guadiana River, following the alert of the disappearance of a 48-year-old man, near Foz de Odeleite, in the municipality of Castro Marim.
In a statement, the authority states that the man of Romanian nationality, who has not yet been found, disappeared during the afternoon, after entering the water and no longer being seen by two fishermen who accompanied him.
The Captain of the Port of Vila Real de Santo António activated a Maritime Police vessel and a vessel from the Vila Real de Santo António Lifeguard Station to the location, after receiving the alert at 4:05 pm through the Search Coordination Center and Lisbon Maritime Rescue.
The searches were interrupted at the end of the afternoon and will resume on Monday, a source from the National Maritime Authority told Lusa.
