Lagoa, Faro, 11 November 2020
JPC//MCL
Body of man found floating in the sea in Lagoa, Algarve
The body of a man appearing to be between 60 and 70 years old was found this afternoon floating in the sea near Estaquinha beach, in the municipality of Lagoa (Algarve), revealed the Maritime Authority.
According to the authorities, the man, a fisherman, allegedly fell into the sea while fishing recreationally on a cliff, on that beach in Lagoa, in the district of Faro.
After an alert was given to the sighting of a body floating in the sea, the Ferragudo Life Saving Station was activated and collected the body, transporting it to the Portimão marina.
“Death was declared on site by the Lagoa health delegate, and the body was transported to the forensic medicine office in Portimão”, specified the Maritime Authority.
The Portimão Maritime Police took charge of the incident.
